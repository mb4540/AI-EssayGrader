## What is EssayEase?

EssayEase is a web-based application that helps teachers grade written assignments quickly and consistently while maintaining the quality and fidelity of traditional grading. Using advanced AI technology, the system evaluates student essays according to **your specific rubric and criteria**, providing detailed feedback that you can review, edit, and approve before sharing with students.
